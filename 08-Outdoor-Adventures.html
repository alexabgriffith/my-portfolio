Outdoor Adventures Magazine Illustration

Illustration
Editorial
This vector-based magazine illustration was developed around the theme of outdoor activities, with a specific focus on rock climbing, mountain biking, and camping. The goal was to visually enhance an editorial article by creating a dynamic and engaging full-page spreads.

I chose a bright, earthy color palette to draw the viewer’s eye while maintaining a natural, grounded aesthetic that complements the outdoor subject matter. This project showcases my ability to convey story through illustration, use color strategically, and design with both impact and purpose in mind.
