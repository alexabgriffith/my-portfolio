Finding Flight

Illustration
Raster-based
This project is a Pixar-inspired animated movie poster that focuses on a single main character. The goal was to tell a meaningful story through visual design by creating a unique style, an emotional tone, and a clear sense of character.

Finding Flight follows a young dragonfly born without wings, trying to find their place in a world full of flying insects. In the poster, the main character is shown looking down at their reflection with wings. It’s a quiet moment that captures the heart of the story: hope, identity, and the dream of belonging.

The poster is meant to spark empathy and curiosity, offering a glimpse into the bigger journey behind the film.
