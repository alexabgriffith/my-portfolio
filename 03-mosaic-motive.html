Beer Can Design

Illustration
Packaging
Client Project
This project was developed for a client of Alter Brewing Co. as part of a design competition involving 40 students. The client's goal was to select one standout design that best aligned with their creative vision. The winning design would be printed on the can by Hart Print.

The objective was to create a bold, visually striking concept—something colorful, loud, playful, and trendy. Aside from mandatory legal information and the Alter Brewing Co. logo, students had complete creative freedom in developing their concepts.
