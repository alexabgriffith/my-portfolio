Biteful

Illustration
Motion Graphics
UI/UX
Logo
In this project, we were tasked with designing a brand illustration system for a business of our choice. I chose to create branding for a healthy meal prep service, which I named “Biteful.”

To reflect the idea of healthy eating and create an inviting atmosphere, I used a vibrant and colorful palette throughout my illustrations. I also introduced a cheerful lemon character to add personality and help build a stronger emotional connection between the brand and its audience.
