Sport Mascot Logo

Logo
Illustration
This project features a mascot logo designed for a college competitive swimming team. I chose a shark as the mascot to symbolize power, agility, and focus—traits that align closely with the spirit of competitive swimming. The goal was to create a bold, memorable mark that conveys strength, determination, and team pride, while also feeling dynamic and energetic in both print and digital formats. The sharp lines and aggressive expression of the shark reinforce the competitive edge and resilience of the athletes it represents.
