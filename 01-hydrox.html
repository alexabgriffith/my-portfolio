Hydrox Water Intake App

Logo
Branding
App Design
Hydrox is a health-focused app designed to help users track their daily water intake and stay hydrated. The goal of this project was to develop a distinctive brand identity that sets Hydrox apart from other wellness and hydration apps.

The logo features a fennec fox, a desert-dwelling animal known for thriving in dry environments with minimal water—an unexpected yet symbolic animal to represent hydration. The visual identity is inspired by a desert theme, emphasizing a unique take on wellness branding.
